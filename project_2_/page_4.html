You are now approaching thirty. You have stable income and work.

Secularly, you are living a well organized and successful life, except for one thing,

Your family calls it your “life’s big matter”.

People started asking if you get a partner, and if you need them to introduce someone.

Once you get a partner, questions shift to when do you guys plan to marry.
Then, to when are you planning to have a children.
The questions never really stop. So you decide to:...

A: Go on blind dates, get married, and start a family.
B: No. I will stay single and live my life without marriage or children.

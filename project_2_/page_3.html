Year pass. You finally graduated. But life never ends here.

People started asking: “So what are you going to do next…?”

Everyone seems to agree that adulthood begins now, and you must quickly choose a direction.

Several paths appear in front of you:
-Prepare for graduate school exams and continue studying.
-Prepare for the civil service exam and pursue a stable government job. (In Chinese culture)
-Enter the job market and start building your career.

Each path promises something: success, stability, respect, security.
Choose wisely:

A: Follow one of the expected paths (graduate school, civil service, or a job).
B: Refuse to choose any of them.

Interrelated Power
Fans

Fans engage in idol culture by projecting intense, often unconditional love onto idols, investing time, energy, and money to gain emotional fulfillment. Supporting an idol’s growth and passion gives fans a sense of happiness and hope, sometimes feeling like buying joy through consumption.
This devotion can also drive fans to develop skills, such as language learning or design.
At the same time, however, fans’ emotions are heavily shaped by idols—their success, struggles, and public image directly affect how fans feel.
In K-pop, idols are constructed as near-perfect figures and even imagined romantic partners, making fans both devoted supporters and emotionally dependent participants in this dynamic.

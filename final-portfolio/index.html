Project 2: HyperNarrative

Concept Document
Final: Life Milestones

This interactive narrative website explores the idea of the “social life cycle” or "milestones", the series of expectations and roles that society assigns to individuals as they grow older. At each stage of life, people are expected to complete certain tasks: study and succeed academically, build a career, form relationships, and follow gendered social norms. These expectations often appear natural and inevitable.

In the project, the audience moves through different stages of life, from childhood to adulthood. At each stage they are presented with two choices: to follow the expected social role or to reject it. Following the rules allows the user to continue progressing through the life cycle toward a “normal” ending. Rejecting them leads to social criticism and failure.

Before this semester, I was unaware of the existence of vibe-coding and was completely unfamiliar with coding. Coding seems so distant with me, as I'm a design and artistic student. However, I started learning basic coding in this class, which made vibe-coding possible for me since I know which part of the code is working for the effects. Throught the readings and artist presentations, I discovered styles I like, for instance Chia Amisola's work and her web coding style.

Ideas became applicable with the help of AI. I often began with a visual or conceptual idea and with Claude or GPT, ideas that would have taken hours to debug could be realized almost instantly. At the same time, it also made me question the authorship, how much of the work is mine versus the AI's. In this new and constantly changing era, I am constantly concerned about the future of us artists.
